Created: 2025-08-07 21:18:11 IST

## Questions

1. What does LASER stand for?
   - A) Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation
   - B) Light Amplified Source Energy Release
   - C) Light And Sound Energy Radiation
   - D) Light Amplified Sonic Energy Radiation
   Correct: A

2. Which component of a laser is responsible for population inversion?
   - A) Optical cavity
   - B) Pumping source
   - C) Active medium
   - D) Output coupler
   Correct: B

3. In a He-Ne laser, what is the wavelength of the most common emission?
   - A) 532 nm
   - B) 633 nm
   - C) 780 nm
   - D) 1064 nm
   Correct: B

4. What type of laser uses semiconductor P-N junction?
   - A) Ruby laser
   - B) CO2 laser
   - C) Diode laser
   - D) Dye laser
   Correct: C

5. Which property of laser light indicates all waves are in phase?
   - A) Monochromaticity
   - B) Coherence
   - C) Directionality
   - D) Intensity
   Correct: B
